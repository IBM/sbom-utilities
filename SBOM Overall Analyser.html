SBOM Executive Summary
🔒
Your data stays private
Your SBOM — including company name, package names, versions, and license data — is processed entirely within your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server. All analysis runs locally in JavaScript. The only external calls made are the optional registry lookups in the Live Retrieval tab, which send only individual package names to public registries (PyPI, npm, etc.) to retrieve license information — no SBOM structure, company identity, or context is transmitted.
